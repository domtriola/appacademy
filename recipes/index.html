Soy Lemon Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Soy Sauce
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Brown Rice
  • Your favorite veggies

Description

A most delicious salmon dish.

WARNING: once you've eaten salmon this way you'll never again be able to fully appreciate salmon cooked any other way. Proceed at your own risk.

Directions

  1. Start brown rice first
  2. Chop garlic 10 minutes before cooking
  3. Preheat oven to 450Â°F
  4. Melt butter in small pot
  5. Add lemon and soy sauce to butter
  6. Bring to light boil and let simmer for ~5min
  7. Lay salmon down on pan in an aluminum foil boat
  8. Add garlic to sauce and pour over salmon
  9. Bake salmon for 12-15min
  10. Cook your veggies while salmon is in oven
  11. Serve salmon with rice and veggies
  12. Enjoy!
